History

I grew up all over the country, in California, North Carolina, Utah, Colorado and Pennsylvania. My favorite part was seeing how different each state was, even in the same country! After high school, I went to BYU-Idaho for a semester then took two years off to serve a full-time mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints in Brazil and Florida. When I got back, I attended BYU and decided to major in Accounting which is what I'm working on now.

Family

I am the 4th of 6 siblings! Now we live spread out all across the country. My sister and her husband live in Oklahoma, my brother and his wife in North Carolina, the rest live in Colorado. I live in Utah! We love visiting each other and going on vacation. This past summer, we all went to Greece.

Greece was a ton of fun!
